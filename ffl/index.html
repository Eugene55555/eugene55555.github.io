FAQ

Frequently Asked

Do FFL holders have to keep acquisition and disposition records?

Yes. Federal law requires licensed firearms dealers to maintain acquisition and disposition records under 27 CFR 478.125.

What does 27 CFR 478.125 require?

Dealers must record, for each firearm acquired and disposed of, the date, name and address of the person, and firearm/serial details in a bound format. This log book is pre-formatted to that layout.

Is this book "ATF approved"?

No log book is individually "approved" by ATF. This book is formatted to match the record layout inspectors look for under 27 CFR 478.125. A compliance tool, not legal advice.

Paperback or Hardcover — which should I get?

Paperback ($13.99) is the everyday write-in log. Hardcover ($20.99) is the durable, archival option — and the best value for a permanent record. Many dealers keep a hardcover master and a paperback working copy.

How many pages is the book?

120 pages, pre-formatted for acquisition and disposition entries with space for dates, names, addresses, and serial numbers.
